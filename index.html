Our Departments

A seamless educational journey from their first day of school through the vital Junior High years.

Preparatory Department

Early Childhood Education focused on building a strong foundation. We utilize play-based and interactive learning to develop early reading, math, and social skills in a nurturing environment.

Explore Prep

Elementary Department

Grades 1 through 6. We foster curiosity and develop core competencies in reading comprehension, mathematics, science, and essential values formation to prepare them for secondary education.

Explore Elementary

Junior High School

Grades 7 through 10. A dynamic 4-year curriculum that challenges students with advanced sciences, mathematics, and critical thinking, preparing them to excel in prestigious Senior High Schools.

Explore JHS

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about enrollment, early education, and JHS academics.

For our Preparatory program (Kindergarten), children must be at least 5 years old by October 31st of the academic year they are enrolling in, in accordance with DepEd guidelines.

Incoming Grade 7 students must submit their original Form 138 (Grade 6 Report Card), PSA Birth Certificate, Certificate of Good Moral Character, and 2 copies of a 2x2 ID picture. An entrance exam is also required.

Transferees must provide their Form 138 (Report Card), a Certificate of Good Moral Character from their previous school, PSA Birth Certificate, and complete the entrance assessment provided by the guidance office.

Elementary classes run from 7:30 AM to 3:00 PM, while Junior High School classes run from 7:30 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Extracurricular activities are held after dismissal.

Yes. All students from Prep to Grade 10 are required to wear the official PHLA High School uniform. PE uniforms are strictly for physical education days. Complete guidelines are available in the Student Handbook.
